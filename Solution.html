Solution Statement

The proposed solution entails the development of a web application, Traffic Tamer, designed to assist users in navigating and understanding traffic laws with ease. Leveraging traffic data and utilizing machine learning algorithms, the application will categorize and present relevant laws based on the user's specified state, county, local police code, and violation code. The application will feature a function to simplify complex laws into layman's terms, enhancing comprehension for users encountering difficulty. The application provides an automated law lookup and a simplified explanation of the relevant laws. If users have additional questions, the application offers guided questions to further clarify and assess their situation, ensuring users can understand the law and decide on their next steps effectively.

What It Will Do

  • Analyze current traffic laws
  • Remain updated with new traffic laws
  • Categorize traffic laws by state
  • Offer simplified explanations of traffic laws to users
  • Output a requested traffic law

What It Will NOT Do

  • Provide legal consultation or aid: The applicationâ€™s purpose is to educate and help its users understand the traffic laws in their area.
  • Provide driving guidelines or advice: It is still up to the users to ensure that they are capable of driving safely.
  • Be a replacement for the established traffic laws: While the application may present traffic laws and make them more understandable, it is not meant to replace driver training.
